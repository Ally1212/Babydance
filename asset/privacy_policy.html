Last updated: 26 May 2025

Your privacy is of the utmost importance to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Peekababy ("we" or "us") collect, use, and disclose information about you when you access or use our mobile application and services (collectively, the "Services"), and when you contact our customer service team or otherwise interact with us.

Collection of Information

We may collect information when you register for an account, use interactive features, make a purchase, or communicate with us. The information you provide may include:

  • Personal details: your name, email address

Information We Collect Automatically

When you use the Services, we collect:

  • Usage information: features you access, content you create
  • Device information: device type, operating system version
  • Log information: IP address, app version, access times

Use of Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our Services
  • Improve and develop new features
  • Process transactions and fulfill orders
  • Respond to customer service requests

Sharing of Information

We may share information about you:

  • With service providers who assist us in operating the Services
  • If required by law or legal process
  • In connection with any merger or sale of company assets

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your account information by contacting us at support@jonlantech.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated version will be indicated by the "Last updated" date.
